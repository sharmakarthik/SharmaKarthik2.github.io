Self Learning Bot

AI-powered virtual assistant for tasks.

GPT-2

Used for generating intelligent and context-aware responses, GPT-2 forms the backbone of the chatbot's ability to understand and interact with users in a natural and conversational manner. Its deep learning model improves the bot's capacity to handle a wide range of queries effectively.

Tkinter

Tkinter is utilized for building the Graphical User Interface (GUI), making it user-friendly and visually appealing. It handles user interactions through an intuitive interface, allowing users to engage with the bot easily.

NLP and Spacy

In this project, Spacy was used as the core NLP library to process and analyze user inputs. Spacy allowed the chatbot to perform tokenization, part-of-speech tagging, and named entity recognition, which helped the bot understand and extract key information from user queries.

To further enhance the bot’s response accuracy, we implemented TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) to assess the importance of words in the context of user inputs. This allowed the bot to prioritize significant terms and better interpret the meaning of sentences.
Additionally, we used Cosine Similarity to measure the similarity between the user’s query and stored responses. By calculating the angle between vectorized representations of text, the bot was able to identify and return the most contextually relevant answers. Together, these techniques significantly improved the bot's ability to process and respond to complex questions.

Speech Recognition & pyttsx3

These two technologies enable voice interaction with the bot, allowing users to speak to the assistant and receive responses vocally. This adds a layer of accessibility and versatility, transforming the chatbot into a voice-based virtual assistant.
